# Native controls

WPF's built-in controls, restyled to match the design system. They need no
special types — a plain `<Button>`, `<TextBox>` or `<ComboBox>` picks up the
themed look as soon as the styles are merged (see [Getting started]({{ site.baseurl }}/#getting-started)).

Most natives are styled implicitly and documented in the **`Joufflu.Samples`**
gallery rather than here; this section covers the ones that expose named style
variants worth spelling out.
